# Amazon Seller Monitoring Checklist
## Daily & Weekly Tasks for 10% ACOS Success

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL - Daily Monitoring (15-30 minutes)

### 1. Ad Performance Metrics
**Where**: Amazon Advertising → Campaigns → Performance Dashboard

**Metrics to Check**:
- [ ] **Total ACOS** (Target: <10%)
  - Overall portfolio ACOS
  - If >15%, identify which campaigns/products are driving it up
  
- [ ] **Daily Ad Spend vs Budget**
  - Ensure campaigns aren't overspending
  - Check for sudden budget spikes (may indicate high-competition events)
  
- [ ] **Impression Share**
  - If dropping significantly, may indicate bid issues or budget constraints
  - Target: 80%+ on key campaigns

**Action Thresholds**:
- **ACOS >20%**: Investigate immediately, reduce bids or pause keywords
- **Spend >Daily Budget**: Adjust budgets or bids
- **Zero Impressions**: Check campaign status, keyword bids may be too low

---

### 2. Inventory & Stock Levels
**Where**: Inventory → Manage Inventory → FBA Inventory

**Critical Checks**:
- [ ] **Out of Stock Products** (Red Alert 🚨)
  - Any product out of stock = STOP ads immediately
  - Out of stock = Lost sales + Higher ACOS on wasted clicks
  
- [ ] **Low Stock Warning** (<30 days supply)
  - Plan reorder before stockout
  - Reduce ad spend gradually if supply is constrained
  
- [ ] **Stranded Inventory**
  - Fix immediately (creates wasted ad spend)
  - Check FBA inventory health daily

**Action Thresholds**:
- **<7 days stock**: Reduce ad budgets 50% (prevent stockout)
- **Out of stock**: Pause all campaigns for that ASIN
- **Stranded items**: Fix listing issues within 24 hours

---

### 3. Buy Box Status
**Where**: Inventory → Manage Inventory → Buy Box Percentage column

**Check**:
- [ ] **Buy Box Win Rate** (Target: >95%)
  - If <90%, check seller performance metrics
  - Losing Buy Box = Lower conversion = Higher ACOS

**Red Flags**:
- Buy Box % drops below 90% → Check order defect rate, late shipments
- If you lose Buy Box → Pause ads until resolved (wasted spend)

---

### 4. Campaign Status & Errors
**Where**: Amazon Advertising → Campaigns

**Quick Scan**:
- [ ] **Paused/Archived Campaigns** (verify intentional)
- [ ] **Campaign Errors/Warnings** (fix immediately)
- [ ] **Budget Exhausted Warnings** (increase if performing well)
- [ ] **Low Bid Warnings** (may not be winning auctions)

## 🟡 IMPORTANT - Weekly Deep Dive (1-2 hours)

### Week 1: Keyword & Search Term Optimization

**Day**: Monday or Tuesday

#### 1. Search Term Report Analysis
**Where**: Reports → Advertising Reports → Search Term Report

**Download**: Last 7 days (or 14 days for more data)

**Analysis Process**:
```
1. Sort by "Spend" (highest first)
2. Identify top 50 terms with:
   - High spend ($10+) but zero orders
   - High ACOS (>20%)
   - Low conversion rate (<1%)
   
3. Add as Negative Keywords (Campaign or Ad Group level)

4. Extract winners:
   - Terms with ACOS <10%
   - Terms with 2+ orders and good conversion
   - Add these as Exact Match keywords in Profit campaigns
```

**Action Items**:
- [ ] Add 20-30 new negative keywords weekly
- [ ] Move 5-10 winning terms to Exact Match campaigns
- [ ] Document top waste terms for future prevention

---

#### 2. Keyword-Level Performance Review
**Where**: Campaigns → [Select Campaign] → Keywords tab

**For Each Keyword**:
- [ ] **ACOS <8%** → Increase bid 10-20% (scale winners)
- [ ] **ACOS >20%** → Decrease bid 20-30% or pause
- [ ] **Zero clicks in 7 days** → Pause or increase bid
- [ ] **High clicks, zero orders** → Add to negatives or reduce bid 50%

**Priority Keywords to Review**:
1. Top 20 by spend (highest impact)
2. Keywords with recent ACOS spikes
3. New keywords added last week

---

### Week 2: Campaign-Level Optimization

**Day**: Wednesday or Thursday

#### 1. Campaign Performance Comparison

**Metrics Table**:

| Campaign Name | Spend | Sales | ACOS | Orders | CVR | Status |
|--------------|-------|-------|------|--------|-----|--------|
| [Campaign 1] | $XXX  | $XXX  | XX%  | XX     | XX% | ✅/⚠️/❌ |

**Status Indicators**:
- ✅ **Green**: ACOS <12%, good volume
- ⚠️ **Yellow**: ACOS 12-20%, needs optimization
- ❌ **Red**: ACOS >20%, immediate action needed

**Actions by Status**:
- [ ] **Green Campaigns**: Increase budget 10-20%
- [ ] **Yellow Campaigns**: Reduce bids, add negatives
- [ ] **Red Campaigns**: Pause or dramatically restructure

---

#### 2. Budget Reallocation
**Strategy**: Shift budget from low-performers to high-performers

**Process**:
```
1. Calculate total ad budget for week
2. Identify budget wasted on high-ACOS campaigns
3. Reallocate to low-ACOS campaigns:
   - Move $X from Campaign A (ACOS 25%) 
   - To Campaign B (ACOS 8%)
4. Update daily budgets
```

**Weekly Budget Adjustment Guidelines**:
- High-ACOS (>20%): Reduce budget 30-50%
- Medium-ACOS (12-20%): Reduce budget 10-20%
- Low-ACOS (<12%): Increase budget 20-30% (if ACOS stays stable)

---

#### 3. Bid Strategy Review

**Check Current Bid Settings**:
- [ ] **Fixed Bids**: Review if should switch to Dynamic (Down Only)
- [ ] **Dynamic Bids**: Check if adjustments are helping or hurting
- [ ] **Placement Adjustments**: Review performance by placement (Top/Rest/Product Pages)

**Bid Adjustment Rules**:
```
IF keyword ACOS < 8% AND spend > $10/week:
  → Increase bid 10-15%
  
IF keyword ACOS > 20%:
  → Decrease bid 20-30%
  
IF keyword spend > $50/week AND ACOS > 25%:
  → Pause keyword (unless strategic)
```

**Day**: Friday or Saturday

#### 1. Product-Level ACOS Analysis

**Product Performance Table**:

| ASIN | Product Name | Ad Spend | Ad Sales | ACOS | Organic Sales | TACOS |
|------|--------------|----------|----------|------|---------------|-------|
| [ASIN] | [Name] | $XXX | $XXX | XX% | $XXX | XX% |

**Actions**:
- [ ] **Products with ACOS <10%**: Increase ad budgets
- [ ] **Products with ACOS >25%**: Review listing quality, pause ads, or optimize
- [ ] **Products with high TACOS**: Focus on growing organic sales

---

#### 2. Listing Quality Audit

**Check Each Top Product**:
- [ ] **Main Image**: Clear, professional, white background?
- [ ] **Title**: Keywords in first 50 characters? Under 200 chars?
- [ ] **Bullet Points**: Benefit-focused? All 5 slots used?
- [ ] **Reviews**: Average rating >4.5? 50+ reviews?
- [ ] **A+ Content**: Live? Complete? Converting well?
- [ ] **Price**: Competitive? (Within 10% of top 3 competitors)

**If Listing Needs Work**:
- Priority 1: Improve main image and first 3 bullets
- Priority 2: Add/replace poor quality images
- Priority 3: Optimize title with better keywords
- Priority 4: Create/improve A+ content

---

#### 3. Review Velocity & Rating Trends

**Monitor**:
- [ ] **Review Count**: Growing steadily? (Target: 5-10% monthly)
- [ ] **Average Rating**: Stable at 4.5+? (Alert if drops below 4.0)
- [ ] **Negative Reviews**: Address quickly (respond within 24 hours)

**Impact on ACOS**:
- Rating drops from 4.5 to 4.0 = ~20% conversion drop = ~25% ACOS increase
- Must maintain quality and customer service

## 🚨 Emergency Alerts (Check Immediately)

### Immediate Action Required When:

1. **Product Goes Out of Stock**
   - ✅ Pause all campaigns for that ASIN
   - ✅ Set inventory alert
   - ✅ Plan restock timeline

2. **ACOS Spikes Above 30%**
   - ✅ Identify offending campaign/keyword
   - ✅ Reduce bids or pause immediately
   - ✅ Investigate cause (competitor, listing issue, etc.)

3. **Buy Box Loss (>10% of products)**
   - ✅ Check seller performance metrics
   - ✅ Review order defect rate
   - ✅ Fix any account health issues

4. **Daily Budget Exhausted Before Noon**
   - ✅ Increase budget if performance is good
   - ✅ Or reduce bids if ACOS is high

5. **Campaign Paused by Amazon**
   - ✅ Review policy compliance
   - ✅ Fix issues immediately
   - ✅ Contact support if unclear

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Time-Saving Strategies

1. **Use Automation Rules** (if available in your tool):
   - Auto-pause keywords if ACOS >25% for 7 days
   - Auto-increase bids if ACOS <8% and spend >$10/week

2. **Create Weekly Templates**:
   - Standardize your review process
   - Use Excel/Google Sheets for calculations
   - Save report formats

3. **Focus on Top 20%**:
   - 80% of spend likely comes from 20% of campaigns/keywords
   - Prioritize optimizing those first

4. **Set Alerts**:
   - Stock level alerts
   - Budget exhaustion alerts
   - ACOS threshold alerts (if using tools)
