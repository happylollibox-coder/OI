# Amazon Search Catalog Performance (SCP) - Complete Guide
## Understanding This Critical Metric for 10% ACOS Success

---

## What is Search Catalog Performance?

**Search Catalog Performance (SCP)** is a dashboard/report in **Amazon Brand Analytics** that shows how your products (your catalog) perform in the Amazon search results funnel. It tracks both **organic + paid (ad) impressions** that appear on search results pages.

### Key Facts:
- **Location**: Brand Analytics → Search Catalog Performance
- **Requirements**: 
  - Professional Seller Account
  - Amazon Brand Registry enrollment
- **Data Scope**: Search results page activity only (does NOT include product detail pages, widgets, or off-search placements)

---

## Key Metrics in Search Catalog Performance

The SCP dashboard shows these critical funnel metrics **per ASIN**:

### 1. Impressions
**What it means**: Number of times your ASIN appears in Amazon search results (both organic and sponsored)

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- Low impressions = Low visibility = Need more ads or better SEO
- If impressions drop suddenly → May indicate listing issues, indexing problems, or competition

**Target**: Should be increasing or stable for top products

---

### 2. Clicks
**What it means**: Number of times shoppers click your product after seeing it in search results

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- Clicks are the first step toward conversion
- High clicks with low conversion = Wasted ad spend = Higher ACOS
- Need to optimize listing quality if clicks don't convert

**Target**: Increasing clicks with stable or improving conversion rate

---

### 3. Click-Through Rate (CTR)
**Formula**: `CTR = (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100%`

**What it means**: Percentage of impressions that result in clicks

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- Low CTR = Listing not compelling in search results
- Poor title, main image, or pricing = Low CTR
- Higher CTR = More clicks per impression = Better efficiency

**Benchmarks**:
- **Good CTR**: 0.3% - 1.0%+ (varies by category)
- **Poor CTR**: <0.2%
- **Excellent CTR**: >1.0%

**If CTR is low, optimize**:
- Main product image (clear, professional, appealing)
- Product title (keyword-rich, benefit-focused first 50 characters)
- Price competitiveness (within 10% of top competitors)

---

### 4. Add-to-Carts
**What it means**: Number of times your product is added to shopping cart after clicking from search

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- Indicates purchase intent and interest
- High add-to-carts but low purchases = Issues with final conversion
- May indicate price, Buy Box, or shipping speed problems

**Funnel Health Check**:
- Good: Clicks → Add-to-Carts conversion: 10-20%
- If low: Review price, shipping options, Buy Box eligibility

---

### 5. Purchases
**What it means**: Completed orders after product page visits via search results

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- This is the goal! Purchases generate revenue
- Higher purchases = Better ACOS (more sales per ad dollar)
- Organic purchases = Lower TACOS

**Target**: Increasing purchases, especially organic (non-ad) purchases

---

### 6. Conversion Rate
**Formula**: `Conversion Rate = (Purchases ÷ Clicks) × 100%`

**What it means**: Percentage of clicks that result in purchases

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- **Most critical metric for ACOS control**
- Higher conversion = Lower ACOS (more sales per click)
- Low conversion = Wasted ad spend = Higher ACOS

**Benchmarks**:
- **Excellent**: >15% conversion rate
- **Good**: 10-15% conversion rate
- **Fair**: 5-10% conversion rate
- **Poor**: <5% conversion rate

**If conversion is low, check**:
- Product detail page quality (images, bullets, A+ content)
- Reviews and ratings (4.5+ stars ideal)
- Price competitiveness
- Buy Box eligibility (must have >90% Buy Box)
- Shipping speed (Prime/FBA helps)

---

### 7. Search Traffic Sales
**What it means**: Total dollar value of sales generated via search (both organic and paid)

**Why it matters for ACOS**:
- Shows revenue contribution from search activity
- Part of TACOS calculation (Total Advertising Cost of Sales)
- Growing organic sales = Lower TACOS

**Target**: Increasing organic search sales as percentage of total

---

## Additional Insights in SCP

### Shipping Speed Performance
Shows impressions, clicks, and purchases broken down by:
- **Same-Day** shipping
- **1-Day** (Prime) shipping
- **2-Day** (Prime) shipping
- **Standard** shipping

**Why it matters**:
- Faster shipping = More impressions and higher conversion
- FBA/Prime products typically perform better
- Use this to decide fulfillment strategy

**Action**: If slow shipping is hurting performance → Consider FBA for faster fulfillment

---

### Price Comparison (Median Pricing)
Shows your price compared to category median for:
- Impressions (where you appear)
- Clicks (where you get clicked)
- Purchases (where you win sales)

**Why it matters**:
- Price significantly above median = Lower conversion
- Price below median = May be leaving profit on table
- Helps with competitive pricing decisions

**Action**: 
- If price too high and conversion low → Adjust price or emphasize value
- If price too low → Test price increases gradually

## How Search Catalog Performance Differs from Advertising Metrics

### Key Differences:

| Aspect | SCP (Search Catalog Performance) | Advertising Dashboards |
|--------|----------------------------------|------------------------|
| **Data Source** | Search results pages only | All ad placements (search, product pages, widgets) |
| **Includes** | Organic + Paid impressions | Paid (ads) only |
| **Focus** | Catalog-level funnel analysis | Campaign/keyword performance |
| **ACOS Shown** | No (doesn't include ad costs) | Yes (shows ACOS directly) |

### Why Metrics May Not Match Exactly:

- **SCP** measures only search results page activity
- **Advertising reports** include all ad placements (detail pages, widgets, etc.)
- Attribution windows may differ slightly
- SCP includes organic impressions; ad reports don't

**Best Practice**: Use both together for complete picture

## How to Use SCP to Achieve 10% ACOS

### 1. Identify Funnel Weaknesses

**Check Each ASIN's Funnel**:

```
Impressions → Clicks → Add-to-Carts → Purchases
```

**Where to Focus**:

| If Problem Here | Likely Cause | Fix |
|-----------------|--------------|-----|
| **Impressions are low** | Poor SEO, wrong keywords, indexing issues | Optimize backend keywords, improve title, check category |
| **CTR is low** (impressions high, clicks low) | Unappealing title/image, price too high | Improve main image, optimize title, review pricing |
| **Conversion is low** (clicks high, purchases low) | Poor listing quality, reviews, Buy Box, shipping | Fix listing content, build reviews, ensure Buy Box, improve shipping |

---

### 2. Optimize Listing Quality Based on SCP Data

**High Impressions, Low CTR** → Fix:
- Main product image (use lifestyle shots, better quality)
- Product title (first 50 characters most important)
- Price positioning (be competitive)

**High Clicks, Low Conversion** → Fix:
- Product detail page (images, bullets, A+ content)
- Reviews and ratings (need 4.5+ stars, 50+ reviews)
- Buy Box eligibility (must maintain >95%)
- Shipping speed (Prime/FBA helps)

**Low Impressions Overall** → Fix:
- Backend search terms (use all 250 bytes)
- Category placement (ensure correct)
- SEO optimization (keyword-rich title/bullets)

---

### 3. Inform Advertising Strategy

**Use SCP to Guide Ad Spend**:

| SCP Metric | Ad Action |
|------------|-----------|
| **High impressions, low CTR** | Pause ads until listing is fixed (wasteful) |
| **High impressions, good CTR, low conversion** | Reduce ad spend, focus on listing optimization first |
| **Low impressions, good CTR, high conversion** | **INCREASE ad spend** - This is a winner! |
| **High organic impressions, good conversion** | Reduce ad dependency, let organic work |

**Budget Allocation Strategy**:
- **Low-ACOS ASINs with high SCP conversion** → Increase ad budgets
- **High-ACOS ASINs with low SCP conversion** → Reduce/pause ads, fix listings first

---

### 4. Monitor Organic vs Paid Performance

**Track Both**:
- **Organic impressions/clicks** → Growing = Less need for ads = Lower TACOS
- **Paid impressions/clicks** → Monitor efficiency (through ad dashboards)

**Goal**: Grow organic search performance to reduce ad dependency

## Daily & Weekly SCP Monitoring

### Daily Quick Checks (5 minutes)

1. **Overall Impression Trends**
   - [ ] Any sudden drops in impressions? (May indicate listing issues)
   - [ ] Top 10 ASINs maintaining visibility?

2. **Conversion Rate Alerts**
   - [ ] Any ASINs with conversion rate drop >20%? (Investigate immediately)

3. **New ASINs**
   - [ ] Are new listings getting impressions? (If not, check indexing)

---

### Weekly Deep Dive (30-60 minutes)

1. **Download ASIN-Level SCP Report**
   - Export data for last 7-14 days
   - Sort by impressions (highest first)

2. **Analyze Funnel Drop-Off**
   - Calculate: Impressions → Clicks → Add-to-Carts → Purchases
   - Identify where each ASIN is losing customers

3. **Compare Top vs Bottom Performers**
   - What do top 20% of ASINs have that bottom 20% don't?
   - (Images? Reviews? Price? Shipping?)
   - Apply learnings to underperformers

4. **Price & Shipping Analysis**
   - Review price median comparisons
   - Check shipping speed performance breakdown
   - Adjust strategy if needed

5. **Correlate with Ad Performance**
   - Cross-reference SCP data with ad campaign performance
   - Identify ASINs where ads are wasteful (low SCP conversion) vs efficient

## Common SCP Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: High Impressions, Zero Clicks
**Problem**: Listing not appealing in search results
**Solution**:
- Improve main product image (professional, lifestyle shot)
- Optimize title (benefit-focused, keyword-rich first 50 chars)
- Review pricing (may be too high vs competitors)

---

### Issue 2: High Clicks, Low Purchases
**Problem**: Listing doesn't convert well (poor detail page)
**Solution**:
- Improve product images (add lifestyle, detail, infographic images)
- Enhance bullet points (benefit-focused, scannable)
- Add A+ Content (if eligible)
- Build reviews (target 4.5+ stars, 50+ reviews)
- Ensure Buy Box eligibility (>95%)

---

### Issue 3: Low Impressions Overall
**Problem**: Poor SEO, wrong category, or indexing issues
**Solution**:
- Optimize backend search terms (use all 250 bytes)
- Ensure correct category placement
- Add primary keywords to title and bullets
- Check for listing errors/suppressions
- Consider ad campaigns to boost visibility initially

---

### Issue 4: Impressions Dropping Suddenly
**Problem**: Listing issue, competition, or algorithm change
**Solution**:
- Check for account health issues
- Verify Buy Box eligibility
- Check inventory levels (out of stock = no impressions)
- Review for policy violations
- Monitor competitor activity

## How SCP Relates to ACOS

### Direct Relationship:

**Better SCP Metrics = Lower ACOS**

Why:
1. **Higher Conversion Rate** → More sales per click → Lower ACOS
2. **Better CTR** → Better relevance → Amazon rewards with lower CPCs → Lower ACOS
3. **More Organic Impressions** → Less need for paid ads → Lower TACOS
4. **Faster Shipping** → More Buy Box wins → Better conversion → Lower ACOS

### Formula Connection:

```
ACOS = (Ad Spend ÷ Ad Sales) × 100%

SCP helps improve:
- Ad Sales (via better conversion rate)
- Organic Sales (via better impressions/clicks)

Better SCP → More sales (organic + paid) → Lower ACOS/TACOS
```

## Accessing Search Catalog Performance

### Where to Find It:

1. **Seller Central** → **Brand Analytics** → **Search Catalog Performance**
   - Requires: Brand Registry enrollment

2. **Alternative**: If not Brand Registered, you can infer SCP-like metrics from:
   - **Advertising Reports** → **Search Term Report** (paid performance)
   - **Business Reports** → **Sales and Traffic** (organic performance estimates)
   - **Inventory Performance Dashboard** (indirect visibility metrics)

### If You Don't Have Brand Registry:

**Get Brand Registry** (if eligible):
- Benefits: Access to SCP, A+ Content, Brand Protection, Amazon Vine
- Requirements: Trademarked brand, brand owner/manufacturer authorization

Search Catalog Performance is **critical** because:

1. ✅ **Shows full funnel** (not just ad performance)
