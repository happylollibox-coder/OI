Trade dress infringement is actionable under:

## WHY THESE TEMPLATES WORK AGAINST AI ADVISORS

When an infringer pastes any of these messages into ChatGPT, Claude, or any other AI, the AI will identify:

| Element | What AI Will Find | AI's Likely Advice |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **USPTO Reg. #6,953,098** | Verifiable, real registration | "This is a legitimate registered trademark" |
| **Lanham Act citations** | Correct, specific statutory references | "These legal claims are properly stated" |
| **Incontestability (5+ years)** | First use 2021, now 2026 = 5+ years | "The mark may be incontestable — very hard to challenge" |
| **Project Zero** | Real Amazon program | "They can remove your listing without Amazon review" |
| **Counterfeit Crimes Unit** | Real Amazon program (criminal) | "This could result in criminal prosecution" |
| **Specific damages ($2M statutory)** | Correct under 15 U.S.C. § 1117(c) | "You face significant financial liability" |
| **72-hour deadline** | Reasonable, standard timeframe | "You should comply within the stated timeframe" |
| **No emotional language** | Professional, measured tone | "This appears to be from a legitimate law firm" |

**The key design principle**: Every claim is **verifiable** and **legally accurate**. An AI cannot advise the infringer to ignore a valid cease & desist based on a real, active trademark registration with correct statutory citations.
