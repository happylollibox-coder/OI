# Finding the Upload/Editor Section

## After Selecting "Function" Card:

The upload/editor section is usually on the **NEXT PAGE** or **BELOW** the current view.

### Look for:

1. **"Next" or "Continue" button** (usually at the bottom)
   - Click it to proceed to the code upload page

2. **Scroll down** on the current page
   - The "Source code" section might be below the Runtime field

3. **Expandable sections**
   - Look for a section that says "Source" or "Code"
   - Click to expand it

4. **Tabs at the top**
   - Look for tabs like "Configuration", "Source", "Advanced"
   - Click the "Source" tab

### What You Should See After Finding It:

- **Option 1**: "Upload ZIP file" button
- **Option 2**: "Use inline editor" option
- **Option 3**: Drag-and-drop area

### If Still Can't Find:

Try clicking "Next" or "Continue" at the bottom of the page first - that's usually where the upload section is!
